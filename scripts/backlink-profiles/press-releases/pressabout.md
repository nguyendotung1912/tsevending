**TSE Vending Revolutionizes Urban Living with Launch of Next-Generation IoT Smart Lockers Featuring Multi-Payment for Ho Chi Minh City Condominiums**

**Ho Chi Minh City, June 18, 2026** – TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company), a pioneering force in smart vending and automated solutions in Vietnam, today announced the launch of its state-of-the-art smart locker solution, specifically designed for condominium complexes across Ho Chi Minh City. This innovative system, available starting in 2026, integrates advanced Internet of Things (IoT) technology and a comprehensive suite of multi-payment options to enhance convenience, security, and efficiency for residents and service providers.

The rapid growth of e-commerce and the increasing demand for secure, convenient package handling in urban residential areas have highlighted a critical need for innovative solutions. TSE Vending's new smart lockers address these challenges directly, offering a robust, user-friendly system that ensures packages are securely stored until retrieved by the intended recipient, minimizing lost items and improving overall resident satisfaction.

These intelligent lockers leverage IoT to provide real-time tracking, remote management, and seamless integration with building management systems. This connectivity allows property managers to monitor locker status, usage patterns, and maintenance needs with unprecedented efficiency. Residents will benefit from unparalleled flexibility and security, with multiple access and payment options including QR code payments, popular e-wallets, credit/debit cards, and even optional biometric authentication. This eliminates the need for physical keys, reduces package delivery complications, and offers secure storage for various items, from parcels and groceries to personal belongings.

"Our vision at TSE Vending has always been to leverage technology to simplify daily life and enhance urban living experiences," said Nguyễn Đỗ Tùng, Co-founder of TSE Vending. "The launch of our new smart locker solution for Ho Chi Minh City's condominiums in 2026 is a significant step towards realizing that vision. We’ve poured years of research and development into creating a system that is not only secure and highly efficient but also incredibly user-friendly and adaptable to the evolving needs of modern residents. With IoT at its core and diverse payment options, we are confident these lockers will redefine convenience and security in residential communities, making daily life easier for thousands of residents and streamlining operations for property management."

The new smart lockers are designed with modularity in mind, allowing for flexible configurations to suit the specific space and needs of any condominium complex. Their sleek, modern design also complements contemporary urban aesthetics. The initial rollout will focus on key residential areas within Ho Chi Minh City, with plans for broader expansion across Vietnam in the coming years.
