**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**TSE Vending Elevates Urban Convenience with Next-Generation IoT Smart Locker Solution for Ho Chi Minh City Apartments**

**TP. Hồ Chí Minh, 18/6/2026** – TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company), a pioneering force in smart vending and technology solutions since 2014, today announced the upcoming deployment of its innovative new generation smart locker solution for apartment complexes across Ho Chi Minh City, set to commence in 2026. This advanced system integrates Internet of Things (IoT) capabilities with diverse payment options, promising unprecedented convenience, security, and efficiency for urban residents and building managements.

Designed to address the growing demands of modern urban living, TSE Vending's smart lockers go beyond traditional storage. Leveraging cutting-edge IoT technology, the lockers offer real-time tracking, remote management, and enhanced security features, ensuring packages and personal items are safe and accessible. This intelligent infrastructure allows building managers to monitor locker usage, status, and maintenance needs remotely, optimizing operational efficiency and reducing manual oversight. The system's flexibility is further amplified by its support for multiple payment methods, including popular cashless options, QR code scanning, mobile applications, and credit/debit card payments, catering to the diverse preferences of Ho Chi Minh City's residents. More information about the smart locker solutions can be found at https://tsevending.com/tu-locker-thong-minh.

The rapid rise of e-commerce has brought significant logistical challenges to apartment management, including package overflow, security concerns regarding deliveries, and the administrative burden on building staff. TSE Vending's smart lockers provide a seamless, automated solution, offering 24/7 self-service access for residents to retrieve packages at their convenience, eliminating the need for manual parcel handling by receptionists or security personnel. This not only streamlines operations for building management but also significantly enhances the resident experience, ensuring secure and timely access to their deliveries, and even offering options for personal storage or item exchange within the community.

Nguyễn Đỗ Tùng, Co-founder of TSE Vending, commented on the launch: "We founded TSE Vending in 2014 with a vision to integrate smart technology into everyday convenience. The introduction of our new generation smart lockers for apartment buildings in Ho Chi Minh City is a significant milestone in that journey. We believe this solution will not only elevate the living experience for urban residents by providing unparalleled security and accessibility for their packages and personal items but also empower building managers with efficient, automated systems. This is more than just a locker; it's a step towards a smarter, more connected urban future for Vietnam."

Since its establishment in 2014, TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company) has been at the forefront of deploying innovative vending and smart technology solutions across Vietnam. With a strong commitment to enhancing daily convenience and operational efficiency, the company continuously invests in research and development to bring cutting-edge solutions to the market. This latest smart locker offering underscores TSE Vending's dedication to modernizing urban infrastructure and improving the quality of life for residents in Vietnam's bustling cities.
