**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**TSE Vending Pioneers Future of Urban Convenience with Launch of Next-Generation Smart Locker Solution in Ho Chi Minh City**

**TP. Hồ Chí Minh, 18/6/2026** – TSE Vending, a leading innovator in smart vending and automated solutions for over a decade, today announced the official launch of its groundbreaking next-generation Smart Locker Solution, specifically designed to enhance urban living in apartment complexes across Ho Chi Minh City. Integrating advanced Internet of Things (IoT) capabilities and diverse multi-payment options, this intelligent system is set to redefine convenience, security, and efficiency for residents and property managers alike.

With the rapid urbanization of Ho Chi Minh City, the demand for streamlined services within residential communities has surged. Package delivery, secure storage, and accessible convenience have become critical pain points for both residents managing busy schedules and property managers dealing with logistical complexities. TSE Vending's new smart locker system directly addresses these challenges, offering a robust, user-friendly, and technologically advanced solution that caters to the modern urban lifestyle.

At its core, the new smart locker solution leverages robust IoT technology, providing real-time monitoring, remote management, and comprehensive data analytics. This connectivity allows for seamless operations, from package drop-off notifications and secure access controls to maintenance alerts and usage reports. Residents will receive instant notifications upon package arrival and can retrieve their items at their convenience, 24/7, using a unique access code or through a dedicated mobile application. The system ensures that valuable deliveries are securely stored, mitigating risks of theft or damage common with traditional mailroom or concierge services.

Users can enjoy unparalleled flexibility with a wide array of integrated payment methods. From popular QR code payment apps and local e-wallets to contactless card options, the system ensures a smooth and inclusive transaction experience for various services, such as renting a locker for temporary storage or paying for specialized deliveries. This multi-payment capability underscores TSE Vending’s commitment to providing accessible and convenient solutions for a diverse user base.

Beyond payments and connectivity, the lockers offer enhanced security features, including robust locking mechanisms, integrated CCTV capabilities for surveillance, and detailed audit trails for every transaction and access event. These features provide peace of mind for both residents and building management, ensuring that stored items are safe and operations are transparent. For more detailed information on the new Smart Locker Solution, please visit https://tsevending.com/tu-locker-thong-minh.

"We founded TSE Vending in 2014 with a vision to integrate smart technology into everyday convenience. The launch of our next-generation smart lockers for HCMC apartments in 2026 is a significant milestone that perfectly embodies this vision," said Nguyễn Đỗ Tùng, Co-founder of TSE Vending. "We've meticulously designed this system to not just solve current logistical challenges but to anticipate future needs, offering unparalleled security, flexibility, and ease of use. This isn't just about storing items; it's about empowering urban dwellers with smarter, more secure, and more convenient living experiences. We believe this solution will set a new standard for residential amenities in Ho Chi Minh City and beyond."

For residents, this translates into ultimate convenience: no more missed deliveries, no waiting for concierges, and secure storage for personal items or online purchases. For property managers, the benefits are equally profound, including reduced operational burdens associated with package handling, enhanced security protocols, and valuable data insights into locker usage patterns to optimize services. The automated system frees up staff time, allowing them to focus on higher-value resident services.

Established in 2014, TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company) has built a strong reputation as a pioneer in innovative vending solutions across Vietnam, known for its commitment to quality, technological advancement, and customer satisfaction. This expansion into smart locker systems is a natural evolution, leveraging the company's decade of expertise in automated solutions to address an emerging market need. The company plans to strategically roll out these smart locker systems across key residential developments in Ho Chi Minh City throughout 2026, with ambitions for broader expansion in the future. Learn more about TSE Vending's commitment to innovation at https://tsevending.com.
