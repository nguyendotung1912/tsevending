**TSE Vending Unveils Next-Generation IoT Smart Lockers, Transforming Package Management and Convenience in Ho Chi Minh City Apartments**

**TP. Hồ Chí Minh, 18/6/2026** – TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company), a pioneering force in automated retail and smart solutions, today announced the official launch of its state-of-the-art, IoT-integrated smart locker solutions designed specifically for residential complexes across Ho Chi Minh City. This new generation of intelligent lockers, set to be widely implemented starting in 2026, promises to revolutionize package management and elevate daily convenience for urban residents.

The rapid urbanization of Ho Chi Minh City and the exponential growth of e-commerce have placed significant strain on traditional package delivery systems within residential buildings. TSE Vending's innovative smart lockers directly address this critical need by providing a secure, efficient, and 24/7 accessible hub for package drop-offs and pick-ups, eliminating the common frustrations of missed deliveries, crowded reception areas, and security concerns.

At the heart of TSE Vending's new system is cutting-edge Internet of Things (IoT) technology, enabling seamless connectivity, real-time tracking, and remote management. This robust infrastructure ensures optimal performance and reliability, offering both residents and building management unparalleled control and insight. Residents will benefit from a highly secure and intuitive interface, accessible via a dedicated mobile application or the locker's integrated touch screen. For a deeper dive into the specific features and benefits, visit https://tsevending.com/tu-locker-thong-minh.

A standout feature of these next-generation lockers is their comprehensive multi-payment integration. Residents can retrieve their parcels and access potential value-added services using a diverse range of payment methods, including QR code scanning, popular e-wallets, and traditional bank cards. This flexibility caters to the modern consumer's preferences, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience.

"We are incredibly excited to introduce our next-generation smart locker solution to the vibrant residential communities of Ho Chi Minh City," said Nguyễn Đỗ Tùng, Co-founder of TSE Vending. "Our goal at TSE Vending has always been to leverage technology to simplify daily life and create smarter urban environments. These IoT-enabled lockers are a testament to that commitment, offering unmatched convenience, security, and flexibility. We believe they will fundamentally change how residents interact with their deliveries, making urban living smoother and more efficient for everyone involved, from residents to building management and delivery personnel."

Since its establishment in 2014, TSE Vending (TSE Technology Joint Stock Company) has been at the forefront of developing innovative automated solutions for the Vietnamese market. This latest offering reinforces the company's commitment to pioneering smart city infrastructure and enhancing user experience across various sectors. With a robust track record in vending and smart solutions, TSE Vending is poised to become a key player in the smart apartment ecosystem, contributing significantly to a more connected and convenient urban landscape.

For more information about TSE Vending and its range of innovative solutions, please visit https://tsevending.com.
